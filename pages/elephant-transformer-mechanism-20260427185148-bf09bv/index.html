Elephant Transformer Mechanism

A playful pseudo-3D mechanism view of the Transformer architecture. Guide a glowing token through the pipeline, rotate the machine, explode the components, and focus on how attention, memory, and generation fit together. The elephant theme turns the model into a machine with ears for multi-head listening, a trunk for contextual reach, and tusks for output projection.

How to explore it

  • Click any stage chip to jump to that mechanism component.
  • Scrub the token phase slider to move information through the model.
  • Use view tilt and exploded mode to inspect structure.
  • Play the loop to watch the architecture breathe like a machine.
